Bag of Joy is a service project initiated and organised by a group of students from Diocesan Boys' School. Through delivering daily essential supplies and treats in what we call “Bag of Joy”, we hope to bring smiles to lives of the vulnerable groups, low-income children and the elderlies, during the COVID-19 pandemic.
We are asking for donations in the form of supplies. The collected Bag of Joy will be distributed to children and elderlies through direct deliveries and community centres. Please join us to spread joy in our community!
